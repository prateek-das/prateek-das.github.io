About this Website

This website was made in 2024 for Mr. Chrzanowski's "Moving the Needle" project for PE/Health at International Academy East.

The "Moving the Needle" project is a project intended to help shift and reevaluate the stigma concerning mental health, since it is a key factor in the lives of mental illness patients. The stigma and the circumstances that stem from this stigma are what determines whether people with mental illnesses reach out for help or decide to face these struggle alone.

This website is only for educational uses only. If you need help, a diagnosis, or an effective solution, please contact a professional or trusted adult to get the help you need.
